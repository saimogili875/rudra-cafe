The Story

Named After the Storm

Rudra takes its name and its trident from the fierce, formless energy of the divine — bold, warm, and a little untamed. We built a space with string lights, open-air seating, and a menu that doesn't take itself too seriously, so you can come for a coffee and stay for the night.

The Setting

Lights On, Doors Open

Two open levels, string lights overhead, and a look that changes completely once the sun goes down. Rudra is built for evenings — low music, warm bulbs, and a mix of open and covered seating.

Whether it's a quick coffee or a full dinner with friends, the space is designed to slow you down a little.
